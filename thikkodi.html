Thikkodi Light House

Thikkodi Lighthouse, located on the scenic coast of Kozhikode, Kerala, is a historic landmark offering panoramic views of the Arabian Sea. Built atop a rocky hill, it stands as a guiding beacon for sailors while providing visitors with stunning vistas of the coastline. Surrounded by coconut groves and serene beaches, Thikkodi Lighthouse is an ideal spot for history buffs and nature lovers alike.
