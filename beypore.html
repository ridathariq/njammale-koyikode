Beypore Port

Beypore Port in Kozhikode, Kerala, is one of India’s oldest ports, historically linking India with the Middle East through trade. Known for its traditional boat-building, especially the iconic uru ships, it remains an active center for fishing and local commerce. Today, it’s also a tourist attraction, celebrated for its cultural heritage and scenic views.
